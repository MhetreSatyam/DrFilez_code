Demo Tour of Website




HINTS



  • 1. Start:
    • Visit our homepage to begin your journey. Click here

  • 2. Registration:
    • If you're new, click on the registration link to provide your details.
    • Complete the registration form with accurate information.
    • Submit the form to create your account.

  • 3. Login:
    • Use your registered credentials to log in.
    • Access your personalized dashboard for a seamless experience.

  • 4. Add Patients
    • After logging in, navigate to the "Add Patients" section.
    • Create a new patient ID by providing the required details.

  • 5. Health Information:
    • For existing patients, log in using your credentials.
    • Search for a patient by ID to access their health information.

  • 6. Contact Us:
    • Find the "Contact Us" link on the homepage.
    • Reach out to us for any queries, assistance, or feedback.

  • 7. Search Specialists:
    • Looking for specialized care? Use the "Search Specialists" option under Doctors tab.
    • Find and connect with the right specialists for your needs.

  • 8. Logout:
    • Ensure secure access by logging out when you're done.

    • Enjoy a seamless experience on our platform! If you ever need assistance, feel free to reach out through our contact channels.
