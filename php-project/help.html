Help

  • How can we access health records on DrFilEZ?


    - To access your health records, log in to your DrFilEZ account and navigate to the 'Health Records' section.



  • Is health information secure on DrFilEZ?


    - Yes, DrFilEZ uses AlgoRand's blockchain network to protect your health data.



  • Should we share health records with healthcare provider?


    - Yes, DrFilEZ allows you to securely share your health records with authorized healthcare professionals.
